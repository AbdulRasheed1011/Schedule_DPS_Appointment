# Schedule_DPS_Appointment
Problem with Scheduling Texas DPS Appointment is, you find the appointment on average after 2 months. So, I've build a AI Agent to book an appointment on your specific date, time might not be the same, in your preferred locations.
Your data is not being stored. Please read the terms and conditions.
Requirements to books an appointment.
1. Your Personal Details. I encourage you to enter 9999 in place of SSN.
2. You will required to enter your OTP to book the appoitment.
3. Finally, Once the slot is find you need to confirm to book the appointment.
